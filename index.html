OUR PROGRAMS

Through the mission of Mighty Oaks Foundation last year, we afforded 749 Warriors the opportunity to attend one of our 32 Legacy Programs, which raises our total program alumni to over 4,000 in the last nine years. Additionally, we reached our goal to serve over 150,000 active duty warriors at Military Resiliency Programs on bases across the nation, while we also distributed over 100,000 Mighty Oaks produced books!
